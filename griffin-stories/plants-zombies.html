Plants vs. Zombies

Story by Griffin, Illustrated by Ray

One day when the zombie was about to go into the house, it ate its own brain.

Then it got bowled over by a bull dozer, and then exploded by exploding cherries. And then it went back into its grave.

Then it sticks its hand out of the grave, and an Angry Bird swings at it screaming an "eeeekkkkkkh," and pops off the Zombie's head and arm.

The End.
