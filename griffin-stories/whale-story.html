A Whale Story

Story by Griffin, Illustrated by Ray

One time Griffin was flying a kite and he tied the kite to the bike.

It made him fly.

He crashed into Levi who was on a glider. His glider broke and he fell into the sea.

He got eaten up by a hump back whale who then poops him out!

Griffin then puts on his backpack, gets out a piece of string and ties it to the humpback whale's fin.

The humpback whale then takes griffin for a ride. The whale gave him a bunch of candy. He ate it all except for the poisin ones, which Levi ate.

The End.
